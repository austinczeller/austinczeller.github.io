Research

I have an interest in using large datasets to understand patterns in ecology using Bayesian statistics.

Interests

Species Habitat Use

Understanding how different species utilize their habitats and the factors that influence distributions are fundamental questions in ecology and have heightened importance during the Anthropocene.

Quantitative Ecology

I have a strong interest in applying quantitative methods to address ecological questions and develop evidence-based management strategies.

Land Management

In a finite world, sustainable land management practices are essential for preserving biodiversity and ecosystem services.

Indigenous Stewardship

Reconceptualizing conservation through the lens of Indigenous knowledge systems and traditional ecological practices is essential for reconciliation and sustainable management.
